January

January 04 - A seed am I, three letters make my name. Take away two and I still sound the same.

January 11 - What can you catch but not throw?

January 18 - People are hired to get rid of me. I'm often hiding under your bed. In time I'll always return you see. Bite me and you're surely dead.

January 25 - I can be written, I can be spoken, I can be exposed, I can be broken.

February

February 01 - Thirty white horses on a red hill, First they champ, Then they stamp, Then they stand still.

February 08 - What is so fragile that saying its name breaks it?

February 15 - They made me a mouth, but didn't give me breath. Water gives me life, but the sun brings me death.

February 22 - What starts with a 'T', ends with a 'T', and has T in it?

March

March 01 - Take off my skin, I won't cry, but you will.

March 08 - I am the yellow hem of the sea's blue skirt.

March 15 - The more of it there is, the less you see.

March 22 - I move without wings, Between silken string, I leave as you find, My substance behind.

March 29 - I'm a god. I'm a planet. I measure heat.

April

April 05 - I look at you, you look at me, I raise my right, you raise your left.

April 12 - What kind of pet always stays on the floor?

April 19 - A time when they're green. A time when they're brown. But both of these times, cause me to frown. But just in between, for a very short while. They're perfect and yellow. And cause me to smile.

April 26 - I'm full of holes, yet I'm full of water.

May

May 03 - The eight of us move forth and back. To protect our king from the foes attack.

May 10 - I build up castles. I tear down mountains. I make some men blind. I help others to see.

May 17 - What do you fill with empty hands?

May 24 - What wears a coat in the winter and pants in the summer?

May 31 - My teeth are sharp, my back is straight, to cut things up it is my fate.

June

June 07 - Runs over fields and woods all day. Under the bed at night sits not alone, With long tongue hanging out, Resting at your feet until we go for a walk.

June 14 - Who is he that runs without a leg. And his house on his back?

June 21 - Give it food and it will live, give it water and it will die.

June 28 - I'm not really more than holes tied to more holes. I'm strong as good steel, though not as stiff as a pole.

July

July 05 - He has one and a person has two. A citizen has three. And a human being has four. A personality has five. And an inhabitant of earth has six.

July 12 - What can be swallowed, But can also swallow you?

July 19 - Always wax, yet always wane: I melt, succumbed to the flame. Lighting darkness, with fate unblest, I soon devolve to shapeless mess.

July 26 - I can be cracked, I can be made. I can be told, I can be played.

August

August 02 - I am the hole in the night, the ever watchful eye. I return in a cycle, to enlighten the sky.

August 16 - What gets bigger the more you take away from it?

August 23 - I dig out tiny caves and store gold and silver in them. I also build bridges of silver and make crowns of gold. They are the smallest you could imagine. Sooner or later everybody needs my help. Yet many people are afraid to let me help them.

August 30 - What word starts with 'E', ends with 'E', but only has one letter? It is not the letter 'E'.

September

September 06 - Die without me, never thank me. Walk right through me, never feel me. Always watching, never speaking. Always lurking, never seen.

September 13 - What has six faces and twenty-one eyes?

September 20 - I have one, you have one. If you remove the first letter, a bit remains. If you remove the second, bit still remains. If you remove the third, it still remains.

September 27 - I'm the source of all emotion, but I'm caged in a white prison.

October

October 04 - I am the red tongue of the earth, that buries cities.

October 11 - Tall in the morning, short at noon, gone at night. But I'll be back soon.

October 18 - A woman shoots her husband, then holds him under water for five minutes. Finally, she hangs him. Five minutes later they enjoy a wonderful dinner together. How can this be?

October 25 - Always wax, yet always wane: I melt, succumbed to the flame. Lighting darkness, with fate unblest, I soon devolve to shapeless mess.

November

November 08 - No matter how little or how much you use me, you change me every month.

November 15 - I wear a red robe, with staff in hand, and a stone in my throat.

November 22 - Today he is there to trip you up. And he will torture you tomorrow. Yet he is also there to ease the pain, when you are lost in grief and sorrow.

November 29 - I have a neck but no head. I have a body but no arm. I have a bottom but no leg.

December

December 06 - A nut cracker up in a tree.
