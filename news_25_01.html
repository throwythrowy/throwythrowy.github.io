January 25

I thought about her more. The lady from the golf club. From her demeanour, she must be from the south, probably even beyond the border. I’ve met many women when I was still in New Polodia, but none like her. In my dreams, we sit together and share thick orange cider, the kind that is somehow both sparkling and syrupy. I’d hide a thin necklace in the bottle, and she would only find out once we’d finished the bottle. She would be so happy, and I would be, too.
