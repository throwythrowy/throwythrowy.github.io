The Daily Regal.

Sad news from Senator Hixton's Mansion. Our favourite feline Big Paws has passed unexpectedly at the tender age of 3 years. Hixton's Staff told us that Big Paws had shown symptoms of dementia in the weeks leading up to his death. Our veterinarian expert tells us that the likely causes of his death are a brain tumor or an aneurysm.
