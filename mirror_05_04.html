April 05

I am preparing for this year's riddle championship. I have a title to defend. For the past 3 years, I have been the reigning king of riddles. Every one of those years I endured a four-hour marathon of the most difficult puzzles gathered over the last decades. This is serious business, and a new contender will participate. Milly Bellington is her name, and she seems very bright. I doubt that she will be on the podium, but she will surely be among the top five.
